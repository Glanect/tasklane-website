Frequently asked questions

Find the answers to some commonly asked questions. Please get in touch if your question is not listed and we'll be happy to help.

What can the platform do?

Our platform is property and portfolio management software. It features a ticketing system and contains pre-built workflows, checklists and audits that help you to ensure high quality standards, and also features portfolio management, space management, monitoring, dashboards, and reporting.

What kind of hardware do I need?

You will need a laptop or desktop PC to access the web version, and a mobile phone or tablet for the mobile app.

How big should my real estate portfolio be?

We can accommodate various sizes, with a minimum of 1 property. Please contact us for more information about your portfolio solution.

How fast can it be implemented?

We only need to upload your floor plans and user list to the platform. The operators can start using the platform immediately.

How much does Tasklane cost?

Every real estate business is unique. Therefore, we need to look at your business to determine the optimal price for your circumstances.
